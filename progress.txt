### Key Learnings
1. Remotion's Sequence calls useVideoConfig() internally — must mock it in scene tests that don't have a <Composition> wrapper. Mock gates children on frameRef.value.
2. jsdom normalizes hex colors to rgb() — test comparisons need to handle both forms (use colorMatches helper pattern).
3. vi.hoisted is necessary (not just module-level let) when the mock factory must close over a mutable variable that Vitest hoists before module-level var declarations are processed.
4. Tile opacity (not mount/unmount) means all tile DOM is always present — AC-DG-7 count is structurally 7 at every frame because both Tile 1 and Tile 3 LiveDecisionsPanes are always mounted.
